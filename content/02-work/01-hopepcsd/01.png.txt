Title: Sermon Page

----

Caption: **Filtering** Users can filter sermon archive the related book or topic category.

**YouTube Implementation** YouTube links of sermon videos are injected into the modal element to minimize HTTP requests at page load. YouTube links are stored locally in each model and are displayed as a collection.

**Infinite Scroll** More sermons are fetched from the server via AJAX when the user nears the bottom of the page.
